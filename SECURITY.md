# Security

TopPaperStyleGuard processes local academic manuscripts and corpora.

Report security concerns by opening a private advisory or issue in the project repository.

Do not upload confidential manuscripts or copyrighted corpora to public issues.

Guardpacks do not store raw source text, but they contain salted hashes that may still reveal whether guessed phrases are present in a corpus. Treat `*.guard.json`, audit reports with `--include-excerpts`, and unpublished drafts as private artifacts.
