# Ethics

TopPaperStyleGuard is for responsible academic writing assistance.

## Intended Use

- Learn structural features of high-quality papers.
- Improve clarity, argument order, and evidentiary restraint.
- Check that agent-assisted drafts do not stay too close to protected source wording.
- Preserve the user's actual claim, method, and contribution.
- Keep guardpacks and unpublished drafts local or private unless the user explicitly chooses otherwise.

## Out of Scope

- Evading plagiarism detection.
- Rewriting copied paragraphs until they pass a detector.
- Fabricating contribution claims.
- Replacing literature review, citation, or methodological judgment.
- Training on papers the user is not allowed to process.

## Practical Rule

If a sentence is flagged, rewrite from the underlying idea and evidence, not by swapping synonyms. The goal is to move farther away from the source, not to disguise proximity.

Do not claim that a clean audit proves originality. It only checks one form of source-like wording overlap.
